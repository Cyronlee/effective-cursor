# Engineering Foundation

> Establish engineering foundation to lay a solid architecture for AI development

## Chapter Goals

In the era of AI-assisted development, many believe they can let AI "freely explore" to build systems. However, practice has shown that **without a solid engineering foundation, AI tends to introduce deviations, technical debt, or low-quality output**.

This chapter introduces how to establish engineering foundations for AI development:
